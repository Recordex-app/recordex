Account & data deletion

Delete your Recordex account

If you want to delete your account and associated data, follow the steps below.

  1. Open the Recordex app.
  2. Go to Settings → Account.
  3. Select Delete account and confirm.

What gets deleted

  • Your account profile and authentication identifiers.
  • App data associated with your account.

Retention

If you have legal/anti-fraud requirements or backups, some data may be retained for a limited period. If you want a specific retention window listed here, tell me what you want it to be.
