Account Deletion

How to permanently delete your data

How to delete your account

  1. Open the Recordex app.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. Confirm deletion.

What happens to your data

  • Your account is permanently deleted.
  • All associated data is permanently removed.
  • No personal data is retained.

Data retention

Recordex does not retain any user data after account deletion. All associated data is permanently and irreversibly removed at the time of deletion.
