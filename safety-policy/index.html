Child Safety & CSAE Policy

Zero tolerance for child sexual abuse or exploitation

Our standard

Recordex has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We prohibit any content or behavior that involves sexual exploitation of minors, including any material that depicts, encourages, promotes, or facilitates CSAE.

What is prohibited

  • Any sexual content involving minors, including images, videos, audio, text, links, or instructions.
  • Content that sexualizes minors (including non-explicit content intended for sexual gratification).
  • Grooming, enticement, or any attempt to exploit or harm a minor.
  • Requests for or distribution of CSAE content, or directions to obtain such material.
  • Any content that promotes, normalizes, or depicts sexual violence or exploitation of children.

Reporting

If you encounter content or behavior that may violate this policy, please report it to us immediately:

  • Email: recordex02@gmail.com
  • Include (if possible): what happened, when it happened, and any relevant screenshots or identifiers.

Enforcement

We may remove violating content, suspend or terminate accounts, and restrict access to the service when we reasonably believe a violation has occurred.

Legal compliance

Where required by law, and when we become aware of suspected CSAE, we will take appropriate action and may report to relevant authorities.
